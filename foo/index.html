How It Works

  • Register: Donors and charities sign up with essential details.
  • List Donation/Need: Donors list available food, charities list needs.
  • Connect: FoodFlow matches donors and charities based on location.
  • Deliver: Donors and charities coordinate pickup or drop-off.

About Us

FoodFlow was created to help reduce food waste by connecting those with surplus food to organizations that can use it. Join us in making a positive impact on society by supporting those in need.
